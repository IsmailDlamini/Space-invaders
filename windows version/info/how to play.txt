

CONTROLS
########

move left -> left key
move right -> right key 
shoot -> space bar


OBJECTIVES
##########

your main objective is to protect the main ship by destroying the meteors on-coming by shooting 
them

-if the meteors get to the main ship the health bar will decrease 
-on event of the health bar reaching zero the game will end 

good luck on playing the game
